Executive Overview

The Women in Executive Leadership Forum offers a carefully designed suite of sponsorship opportunities. Each tier has been crafted to deliver genuine strategic value — not just logo placement, but real access, real relationships, and real impact.

Africa's most prestigious women's leadership gathering bringing together 200-250 senior women executives

About the Forum

The Women in Executive Leadership Forum 2026 brings together 200-250 of South Africa's most influential women executives for a day of inspiration, connection, and strategic dialogue.

  • Senior women executives from leading organizations
  • C-suite leaders and board members
  • Entrepreneurs and business owners
  • Government and policy leaders
  • Rising stars in executive leadership

Why Sponsor?

Position your organization at the forefront of women's leadership development in Africa. Our sponsorship packages offer:

  • Direct access to senior decision-makers
  • Brand positioning as a champion of women's advancement
  • Speaking and thought leadership opportunities
  • Year-round media partnership benefits
  • Meaningful networking and relationship building

Important Notes on Pricing & Terms

Key Terms & Conditions

  • All investment figures are exclusive of VAT. VAT at the prevailing rate (currently 15%) will be added to all invoices.
  • A 10% Early Commitment Discount applies to sponsors who sign and pay the first 50% instalment by 31 July 2025. This discount is applied to the total investment before VAT.
  • Investment figures are firm for the 2026 Forum and will not be subject to mid-year revision. 2027 pricing will be announced separately.
  • Bespoke packages are available for organisations whose objectives do not map neatly to a standard tier. Contact Executive Leadership Club to discuss a custom arrangement.
  • Where multiple Category Sponsorships are acquired by the same organisation, a combined discount of 10% will apply.
  • Executive Leadership Club reserves the right to decline any sponsorship application that is inconsistent with the Forum's values, mission, or delegate community.
  • Payment terms: 50% due on signing of the Sponsorship Agreement; 50% due no later than 30 days prior to the Forum date.
  • All prices are quoted in South African Rand (ZAR) and are valid for 60 days from the date of this document.
